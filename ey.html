ernst & young

At EY, I worked with the Design team on an engagement to design a banking platform for a large financial services client. Coming onto the engagement part way, I was able to look at existing designs and provide feedback on how these designs could be improved. I was also tasked with designing and creating wireframes for certain aspects and pages in responsive platforms. I took ownership in consolidating wireframes into a single Sketch file, and created an interactive prototype in Invision to be presented in client meetings.

Tools Used: Sketch, Invision, Zeplin
