master's thesis (in progress)

Overview

I am starting the journey for my Master's Thesis this semester. This project is in progress, and you can follow my process through this page as I continue to work on and develop my thesis project.

Tools Used (so far)

Card Sorting


Brainstorming

This semester, I am beginning the process of identifying, researching, and prototyping for my Master's Thesis. I first went through a card sorting and mind mapping activity to brainstorm possible topics that I would be interested in diving into. For the card sorting activity, I first identified any sources of inspiration--things that I have been interested by or articles that I have been reading. After this, I went at least three levels into why this was interesting to me. After this, I selected five different topics and asked 6 questions for each topic. With this exercise, I have narrowed my topic down to "menstrual hygiene in homeless women."


Research

I am currently in the process of researching more in depth into this topic to narrow down the specific problem I would like to tackle and the specific goals that I would like to achieve with this project. With high level research, I have identified that I would like to focus on the sections of creating better accessibility to menstruation products, developing more sustainable products, or increasing awareness of the problem.
